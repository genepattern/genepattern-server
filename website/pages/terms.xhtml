In order to use the public GenePattern server, you must first complete the registration form. During registration you are required to give contact information (name, email address). We will not share the information you provide with anyone else or use it to send information to you about anything not directly related to GenePattern.

Jobs that you run on this GenePattern Web Client are submitted to the GenePattern server hosted at the Broad Institute. The server stores the input files, parameter values, and results of the jobs that you run on this site. We do not share this information with other third parties. We may aggregate and analyze log file and database information to determine how the GenePattern software is being used in order to enhance its performance and functionality, including examining jobs and results.

You may opt-out of these communications by choosing to unsubscribe from the mailing list.
